Manual cash terminal

The device enhances visitor interaction and simplifies payment processing for operators

Key Features

Accepting payment for single parking sessions
Displaying the current card balance
Topping up the card balance
Purchasing parking subscriptions
Updating cardholder data
Printing Z-reports

Description

We have developed a dedicated application for this
device, designed to perform the following tasks:

The Kutech Handheld Mobile CashRegister weighs just 460 g, making it comfortable to hold and operate.
It is ideal for processing parking fee payments, whether through a parking attendant or at the facility’s reception desk.
Equipped with Wi-Fi and a SIM card slot, it can connect to the internet from anywhere.
The fiscal drive is easy to install and can be purchased as part of the bundle.
